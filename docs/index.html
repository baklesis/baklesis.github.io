About the Festival

PT Shorts is a celebration of innovative and passionate independent filmmakers from around the world. We showcase groundbreaking short films that challenge perspectives and tell unique stories.

Festival Details

PT Shorts - Potidaneia Short Film Festival is an annual event dedicated to discovering and promoting independent cinema. Our mission is to provide a platform for emerging filmmakers to showcase their creativity and connect with a global audience.

Key Information

  • Date: July 19-20, 2025
  • Location: Potidaneia, Greece
  • Categories: Narrative, Documentary, Experimental, Animation
  • Awards: Best Film, Audience Choice, Best Director, Best Actor, Best Screenplay

Festival Highlights

  • Film Screenings
  • Q&A Sessions with Filmmakers
  • Workshops and Masterclasses
  • Networking Events
  • Awards Ceremony
